NABL Entry Level Accreditation

National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories - Ensuring Quality in Diagnostic Testing

What is NABL Entry-Level Accreditation?

Diagnostic laboratories play a critical role in patient care, as nearly 70% of medical decisions are based on laboratory test results. Accuracy, reliability, and consistency in testing are therefore essential. NABL Entry-Level Accreditation helps laboratories in India establish standardized quality systems and build trust among patients, doctors, and regulatory authorities.

MAGNET SOLUTIONS provides expert NABL Entry-Level Accreditation consultancy, helping pathology and diagnostic laboratories achieve compliance with national quality standards through a practical and systematic approach.

The NABL Entry-Level Accreditation is a certification program introduced by the National Accreditation Board for Testing and Calibration Laboratories (NABL), under the Quality Council of India (QCI). It is designed as a stepping stone for laboratories beginning their accreditation journey.

The program focuses on implementing basic quality management systems as per:

  • ISO 15189 (Medical Laboratories)
  • ISO 17025 (Testing & Calibration Laboratories – applicable scope)

Entry-Level Accreditation ensures that laboratories follow standardized procedures, maintain quality control, and deliver accurate test results.

Key Objective: To encourage laboratories to implement basic quality and safety protocols, creating a culture of continuous improvement from the ground up.

Who Should Apply for NABL Entry-Level Accreditation?

NABL Entry-Level Accreditation is ideal for:

  • Standalone pathology laboratories
  • Diagnostic laboratories
  • Small and medium-sized labs
  • Newly established laboratories
  • Labs planning to apply for NABL Full Accreditation

It is especially beneficial for labs that want to improve quality without immediately facing the complexity of full accreditation.

Scope of NABL Entry-Level Standards

NABL Entry-Level standards focus on essential laboratory quality elements, including:

Laboratory Management System

  • Organization and management
  • Quality management system
  • Document control

Sample Management

  • Sample collection, handling, and storage
  • Pre-examination processes
  • Sample identification and traceability

Equipment & QC

  • Equipment calibration and maintenance
  • Internal quality control (IQC)
  • Proficiency testing

Staff & Ethics

  • Staff competency and training
  • Corrective and preventive actions
  • Patient confidentiality and ethics

Our NABL Entry-Level Accreditation Process

MAGNET SOLUTIONS follows a structured and lab-friendly approach to accreditation.

1

Laboratory Gap Assessment

We assess your laboratory's current practices against NABL Entry-Level requirements to identify gaps in documentation, equipment management, quality control, and staff competency. A clear action plan is created.

2

Documentation Development

We prepare complete NABL documentation, including quality manual, SOPs for tests and processes, equipment logs, QC records, forms, and registers. All documents are customized to your lab workflow.

3

Staff Training & Competency

We train lab technicians and pathologists on NABL requirements, quality control practices, sample handling and reporting, and documentation discipline. Competency assessment methods are also implemented.

4

Quality Control Implementation

We support implementation of internal quality control (IQC), external quality assurance (EQAS) guidance, equipment calibration schedules, and error tracking with corrective actions.

5

Internal Audit & Mock Assessment

Internal audits and mock assessments are conducted to ensure full preparedness before NABL assessment.

6

NABL Application & Assessment Support

We assist with NABL application submission, assessor coordination, and on-site assessment support until accreditation is granted.

Benefits of NABL Entry-Level Accreditation

Achieving NABL Entry-Level Accreditation provides significant benefits to laboratories, patients, and the healthcare system.

Improved Test Accuracy

Standardized procedures reduce errors and improve diagnostic reliability.

Increased Doctor & Patient Confidence

Accreditation builds trust among referring doctors and patients.

Regulatory Compliance

Ensures compliance with national quality and safety standards.

Better Laboratory Management

Clear SOPs improve workflow and accountability.

Pathway to NABL Full Accreditation

Creates a strong foundation for advanced accreditation.

Competitive Advantage

Accredited labs stand out in a competitive diagnostic market.

Challenges Faced by Non-Accredited Laboratories

Labs without accreditation often face:

  • Inconsistent test results
  • Poor documentation
  • Equipment calibration issues
  • Loss of referrals
  • Limited growth opportunities

NABL Entry-Level Accreditation addresses these issues systematically.
