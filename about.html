Who We Are

Magnet Solutions is an all-inclusive healthcare consulting firm, dedicated to the highest standards of care and seamless procedure across India. We partner with hospitals, clinics, and diagnostic facilities, transforming complex regulatory challenges into clear paths for operational and quality excellence.

We provide end-to-end support across two critical areas that drive a modern, compliant, and profitable healthcare facility:

1. Quality & Compliance Consulting
1.1 NABH Entry-Level Accreditation

Complete support for NABH Entry-Level certification, including documentation, SOP implementation, staff training, internal audits, and inspection readiness.

1.2 NABH Full Accreditation

End-to-end consultancy for NABH Full Accreditation with gap analysis, policy formulation, quality indicators, patient safety systems, and final assessment preparation.

1.3 QCI – ECHS Empanelment

Professional assistance for ECHS empanelment under QCI, covering documentation, application processing, compliance implementation, and inspection clearance.

1.4 QCI – CGHS Empanelment

Step-by-step guidance for CGHS empanelment ensuring adherence to QCI standards, statutory compliance, and successful approval.

1.5 TPA Empanelment Services

Complete onboarding support with insurance TPAs, including documentation, tariff structuring, process alignment, and operational readiness.

1.6 NABL Accreditation for Laboratories & Diagnostics

Comprehensive NABL consultancy for labs and diagnostic centers, including quality management systems, technical documentation, internal audits, and assessment support.

2. Integrated IT Solutions
Beyond compliance, we future-proof your facility by offering specialized IT services designed specifically for the healthcare sector. This includes:

  • Digital Marketing: Strategic campaigns to build your brand and increase patient footfall.
  • Website Development: Creating professional, compliant, and user-friendly hospital websites.
  • Hospital Software: Implementation and management of robust Hospital Information Systems (HIS) and Electronic Medical Records (EMR) to streamline operations and enhance patient care.

We operate with the spirit of quality improvement, patient safety, operational excellence, and compliance, ensuring a holistic approach that elevates both your clinical standards and your digital presence.
